Founder

Through customizing workouts and movement practices, I help active people get out of pain without having to give up what they’re passionate about. There are a lot of myths out there that need debunked - like that you have to stop doing what they you to get out of pain or that you need injections, surgery or medication to get out of pain and it’s not true! I’ve helped professional sports organizations, 80-year-old runners, traveling artists and everyone in between. I believe everyone is an athlete and everyone deserves the tools to feel and train like one.

If you are struggling with pain that's preventing you from staying active, reach out and schedule a strategy call today.
